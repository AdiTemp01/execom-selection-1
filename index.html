Dynamic Pricing System

For an Online Marketplace

Problem Statement

An online marketplace wants to introduce a smart dynamic pricing system. The price of a product will increase when demand is high and decrease when demand is low, considering multiple factors such as demand, stock availability, and competitor prices.

Your task is to implement a program that dynamically adjusts the price of a product based on the given parameters.

Input Parameters

Base Price

The starting price of the product before any adjustments.

Stock Availability

Current quantity of the product in inventory.

Number of Views

How many times the product was viewed in the last hour.

Number of Purchases

How many times the product was purchased in the last hour.

Competitor Prices

The prices of similar products from competitors.

Time of the Day

Current time which may affect buying patterns.

Requirements

  • Use any programming language of your choice (C, Java, etc.).
  • Ensure that all the input parameters are considered.
  • Write clean and well-commented code.

Evaluation Criteria

  • Correctness of the algorithm
  • Optimization
  • Scalability
  • Edge Cases
